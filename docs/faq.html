FAQ

"My alarm didn't fire."

Some phones aggressively stop background apps (Xiaomi/Huawei/OnePlus battery optimization). You may need to whitelist ReMind manually via system settings.

"I don't see notifications."

Re-enable the notification permission via App info → Notifications.

"Why does my exported spreadsheet show an apostrophe before some titles?"

Titles that look like formulas are protected so spreadsheet apps don't execute them.

"Where is my data stored? Is there an account?"

ReMind is on-device only. No serverside analytics, no logins, just you and your phone.

"What happens when I reboot my phone?"

Alarms are restored automatically; a pending snooze re-alerts shortly after boot.

"A monthly reminder is set for the 31st. What happens in February?"

ReMind will automatically clamp it down to the last day of the month, so in this case, February 28th.
